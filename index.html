About

I grew up in the tropical Andes, exploring and making my own way to higher summits, between cloud forests and snow fields. I felt drawned to nature, biology and the ecosystems since very young, that's why I studied Forestry Engineering in the University of the Andes and before graduating I was already working in the lab and in the field, getting closer to forest and high altitude environments.

I've been part of incredible ecotourism and nature conservation projects, you can see more at Arawato Adventures and UGAM.

One thing to know about me is this: I genuinely enjoy to constantly updating and acquiring new knowledge.

If I had to list 5 things I love, they would be: mountains, computers, books, guitars, and dogs.

Languages: English (C1) Spanish (native)
