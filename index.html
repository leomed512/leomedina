About

I grew up in the tropical Andes, exploring and making my own way to higher summits, between cloud forests and snow fields. I felt drawned to nature, biology and the ecosystems since very young, that's why before graduating I was already working in the lab and in the field, getting closer to forest and high altitude environments.

I've been part of incredible ecotourism and nature conservation projects, working as a mountain guide and technical advisor for environmental projects, such as the Union of Adventure and Mountain Guides (UGAM). You can see more on my Instagramand UGAM's officialprofile and website.

One thing to know about me is this: I genuinely enjoy to constantly updating and acquiring new knowledge. Currently I'm learning PostgreSQL and French.

If I had to list 5 things I love, they would be: mountains, computers, books, guitars, and dogs.
