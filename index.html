About

I grew up in the breathtaking tropical Andes. From Forestry Engineering, I discovered the amazing power of data. Thereby I'm passionate about exploring the world through data and developing smart solutions that integrate technology with sustainable interactions with nature.

I've also been part of amazing ecotourism and nature conservation projects, working as a technical advisor in Union of Adventure and Mountain Guides (UGAM). You can see more on UGAM's official website and my Instagram page as well.

Languages: English (C1) Spanish (native)

Forestry Engineer

Experienced in geospatial analysis for natural resource planning and management, specializing in map creation for decision-making.

Additionally, I have conducted floristic and topographic surveys and published several scientific research papers in botany, leveraging GIS and data analysis to collect and present information.

Data Analyst

I have experience in acquiring, cleaning, and analyzing data to extract valuable insights through statistical analysis. I use Python (Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn), SQL, and Excel, adapting to project needs. Additionally, I create interactive dashboards with Looker and Power BI to deliver clear, data-driven solutions.

In the frontend field, I have built responsive web interfaces, developed reusable components, and optimized accessibility and performance using JavaScript, HTML5, and modern CSS frameworks.
